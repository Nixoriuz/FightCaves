Recommended Setup

Your Gear (Ironman Setup)

This is a solid setup. The Rune Crossbow with broad bolts will be effective. The Dragonhide body and chaps provide good ranged defense, and the Helm of Neitiznot is a great choice for its balanced stats.

Inventory & Potions

Here's what you should bring. As an Ironman, resource management is key.

Prayer Potions (10-12)

Super Restores (2-3)

Pineapple Pizzas

Karambwans (Optional)

Dashing Kebbits (Run)

Black Chins (Healers)

Rock cake/locator orb

Here's a more accessible setup. Bring plenty of Prayer Potions. Use Pineapple Pizzas for healing. Dashing Kebbits are a good alternative that also restore run energy. Karambwans are great for combo-eating. For Jad's healers, bringing Black chinchompas to throw can quickly tag all of them due to the AoE damage. Use the Rock Cake to lower your health before the caves to save food.
